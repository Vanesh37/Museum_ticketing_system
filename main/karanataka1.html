Gol Gumbaz

Gol Gumbaz, also written Gol Gumbaz, is a 17th-century mausoleum located in Bijapur, a city in Karnataka, India.
It houses the remains of Mohammad Adil Shah, seventh sultan of the Adil Shahi dynasty, and some of his relatives.
